A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, full name Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, was an eminent Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Kalam was known as the "People's President" due to his humble and approachable nature.
